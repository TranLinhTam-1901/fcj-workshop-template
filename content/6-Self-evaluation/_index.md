### Needs Improvement

* **Enhancing Discipline and Compliance with Organizational Regulations:** 
  * Strictly adhere to working hours, enterprise information security regulations, and the general operational workflows of the department.
  * Elevate personal accountability in time management, ensuring that assigned tasks are completed within deadlines, and proactively report work progress to Mentors/Leaders periodically without requiring reminders.

* **Improving and Optimizing Problem-solving Skills:**
  * Cultivate the habit of thoroughly analyzing business requirements and designing clear architectural workflows before writing source code, rather than coding purely by instinct.
  * Learn how to break down complex architectural problems or system bugs into smaller, manageable components to resolve them thoroughly. 
  * Simultaneously, enhance the capability to self-research technical documentations and troubleshoot errors independently before seeking external assistance.

* **Cultivating Communication Skills and Situational Handling in Work and Daily Life:**
  * Improve presentation and explanation skills to convey technical issues or propose solutions clearly, concisely, and understandably to both technical team members and non-technical departments.
  * Learn to actively listen and absorb feedback or constructive criticism from seniors' Code Reviews with a growth mindset.
  * Take more initiative in interacting and connecting with colleagues to integrate smoothly into the corporate culture, thereby improving team collaboration (Teamwork) efficiency.
